Nearly every organization recognizes the need to build a data driven culture. However, many struggle to use data for a variety of reasons, a big one of them being that data exists in all sorts of places. This platform is meant to address that challenge.

This platform has been created as the Foundations central repository for storing and reporting on final data sets and other data types. This data typically originates from grantees, partners, and staff, and then select data types are uploaded into the data warehouse for long-term storage and analysis. Specific guidance on what should be uploaded may be found on Horizon. (Link to a post). Other shared data should be kept in units box folders, or other designated locations. This platforms storage is structured such that staff, grantees, and partners can securely access and analyze the data according to their needs using Power BI, Qualtrics, or other toolsets.
